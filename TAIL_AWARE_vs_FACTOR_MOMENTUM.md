# Tail-Aware Residual Alpha vs ETF Factor Momentum

## Bottom line

Tail-Aware Residual Alpha is the stronger **research-judgement** project.  
ETF Factor Momentum is the more directly **investable empirical case study**.

They should be presented as complementary rather than substitutes.

---

## Factor Momentum

Best description:

> An ETF factor-timing alpha-validation case study.

Strengths:

- tradable ETF implementation;
- realistic execution lag;
- cost proxies;
- SPY benchmark;
- random-selection nulls;
- paired bootstraps;
- spanning regressions;
- walk-forward selection;
- White/SPA multiple-testing controls.

Best-supported result:

> a modest defensive timing effect, especially on drawdown-adjusted metrics, but not a robust standalone alpha.

Main limitation:

> much of the behavior is explained by static factor/market exposures; SPA and spanning weaken the standalone-alpha claim.

---

## Tail-Aware Residual Alpha

Best description:

> A risk-aware alpha-referee / alpha-validation framework.

Strengths:

- original research question;
- direct bridge from risk modelling to alpha research;
- four-state verdict rule;
- hidden crash-risk and hidden-luck diagnostics;
- synthetic validation with known ground truth;
- public-data applications that reject weak signals;
- honest negative-result framing.

Best-supported result:

> the framework is conservative enough to avoid certifying noise or hidden crash-risk premia as genuine alpha, and it rejects weak public-data residual signals rather than overclaiming.

Main limitation:

> no certified empirical alpha yet on public data.

---

## Which is stronger?

| Criterion | Winner |
|---|---|
| Tradable empirical implementation | Factor Momentum |
| Positive realized result | Factor Momentum |
| Originality | Tail-Aware |
| Link to risk background | Tail-Aware |
| Quant Research / Systematic Investing positioning | Tail-Aware |
| Recruiter readability | Factor Momentum |
| Interview depth | Tail-Aware |
| One-page CV if only one alpha project | Depends on target |

Recommendation:

- Use **Factor Momentum** when you want to show an investable systematic strategy implementation.
- Use **Tail-Aware Residual Alpha** when you want to show alpha research judgement, hidden-risk awareness and methodological maturity.
- For QR/Systematic roles, Tail-Aware has higher upside once packaged cleanly.
